OUR SERVICES

Complete Blood Count (CBC)

The Complete Blood Count (CBC) is a common blood test that evaluates the overall health of a patient. It measures various components of the blood, including red blood cells, white blood cells, and platelets. This test helps diagnose a wide range of conditions, including infections, anemia, and blood disorders.

Cost: Rs. 500/-

Blood Glucose

The Blood Glucose test, also known as the blood sugar test, measures the level of glucose (sugar) in the blood. It is commonly used to diagnose and monitor diabetes, a condition characterized by high blood sugar levels. This test is also important for assessing overall metabolic health and detecting hypoglycemia (low blood sugar) and hyperglycemia (high blood sugar).

Cost: Rs. 350/-

Liver Function Test

The Liver Function Test (LFT) measures various enzymes, proteins, and substances in the blood to assess the health and function of the liver. It helps diagnose liver diseases such as hepatitis, cirrhosis, and fatty liver disease. The LFT also evaluates the effectiveness of treatments and monitors liver function during medication use.

Cost: Rs. 1000/-

Kidney Function Test

The Kidney Function Test assesses the health and function of the kidneys by measuring various markers in the blood and urine. It helps diagnose kidney diseases such as chronic kidney disease, kidney stones, and urinary tract infections. This test also evaluates electrolyte levels and the filtration rate of the kidneys.

Cost: Rs. 1500/-
